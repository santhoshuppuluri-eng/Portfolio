Projects

Gesture to Speech Smart Hand Glove Sept 2019, CMRIT.
Role : Team Lead

About nine billion people in the world are deaf and dumb.This creates a very little room for them with communication being a fundamental aspect of human life. These blind people can talk freely by means of normal language whereas the deaf-dumb have their own manual-visual language known as sign language. The project aims to facilitate people by means of a glove based communication interpreter system.

Density Based Traffic Light System May 2019, NIT.
Role : Circuitry Expert

Nowadays, controlling the traffic becomes major issue because of rapid increase in automobiles and also because of large time delays between traffic lights. We have used IR sensors to measure the traffic density. We have placed one IR sensor for each road; these sensors always sense the traffic on that particular road. All these sensors are interfaced to the microcontroller. Based on these sensors, controller detects the traffic and controls the traffic system.

Wireless Power Trasmission Mar 2018, SITE.
Role : Team Lead

In this project, we present the concept of wireless power transmission for multiple devices like cell phones, household appliances, mp3 players, computers, cars, traffic signals and other portable electronics. Our project gives an effective, high performance techniques which can efficiently transmit the power to the required area varying in distances.
